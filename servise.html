Servise19

One of the biggest projects that I developed. servise19 if an Android-IOS app dedicated to the whole Tunisian people. During the Covid-19 pandemic, a lot of handy workers lost their jobs and become unemployed despite their responsibilities and family charge. The role of servise19 is to make the operation of looking for a worker very easy. every handy work should create an account and add the required information and everyone who needs a worker he just launches Service and looks for the nearest worker. the description is very tall but I invite you to try it and discover the amazing features by yourselves.
